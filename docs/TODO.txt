Planned future features, in no particular order.

- Add Exemel::Query, for jQuery/CSS and XPath 1.0 selectors.

  ## assuming $xml is an Exemel::Element or Exemel::Document object.
  my $xq = Exemel::Query.new($xml);

  ## jQuery/CSS style selector is called select().
  ## Selects all <input/> elements with a 'type' of 'radio'.
  my @inputs = $xq.select('input[type="radio"]');

  ## XPath 1.0 selector is called findnodes().
  ## Selects all <title> elements, with a 'lang' of 'en'.
  my @titles = $xq.findnodes("//title[@lang='en']");

- Add Exemel::Formatter, pretty printing and more.

  ## options may be set using special methods,
  my $formatter = Exemel::Formatter($xml);
  say $formatter->indent(2)->collapse;

  ## or during construction.
  my $formatter = Exemel::Formatter($xml, :indent(2), :collapse);
  say $formatter;
